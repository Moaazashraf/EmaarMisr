An Award-Winning Beauty

Winner of “Clubhouse of the Year” award from world-renowned Golf Inc. Magazine, the Uptown Golf Clubhouse is reminiscent of a rich, earthy Spanish palette of colors and textures. Warm and elegant, the various venues of the Clubhouse are perfect for all events; the Uptown Golf Clubhouse has it all.

Sky-high Luxury

At 200 meters above sea level, revel in the heart of Cairo with Uptown Cairo’s dazzling horizon. Cairo’s dazzling skyline paints an epitome of the utmost luxurious cosmopolitan life. Bask in elegance in Uptown Cairo’s elevated and mesmerizing panorama.

Uptown Cairo at a Glimpse

Uptown Cairo’s signature 18-hole golf course invites pleasant greenery in the unique setting of the community, as verdant landscapes merge with the rocky valleys and the elevations with the hillside. Uptown Cairo’s golf course provides an improved, authentic golf experience with all its grandeur.

Roam Like Uptowner

Across vibrant pedestrian trails and lush greenery, Uptowners will find every walking or cycling experience in Uptown Cairo refreshing and rejuvenating.

With easy access from Downtown and central districts of Cairo, Emaar Drive is accessible from everywhere. Now with Emaar Drive and the East Road, Uptowners can reach the east and west ends of the city easily, as well as connecting directly with the 6th of October Bridge. The second phase of Emaar Drive is planned to connect Uptown Cairo to Mehwar El Shaheed, directly gaining access to and from New Cairo.
